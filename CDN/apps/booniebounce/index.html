Boonie Bounce - the new MovieStarPlanet game for iPhone and iPod Touch is finally here!

Want the Boonie as your new best friend?

Help your Boonie bounce as high as you can! Bounce off dots, StarCoins and Fame Stars to try and break your record. If you fall you can save the Boonie with a Bouncy Castle. Unlock the new worlds: MONSTER CASTLE, GREEN HILLS and NEON PARTY. MovieStarPlanet players can transfer the StarCoins and Fame earned to their movie star.

But wait... Something is chasing you! Evil Skulls try to take away your speed, Fame and Star Coins. You really need to avoid these evil things!
